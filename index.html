Gizlock

  • Zero Footprint: No servers, no accounts, and no databases.
  • Total Privacy: Mathematically unbreakable AES-256 encryption.
  • Censorship-Resistant: The content lives completely offline inside the link.

How it works: Gizlock is a completely static tool built entirely on standard HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no backend, no database, no data ever sent or received. Everything happens directly inside your own browser. When you write a page, your device generates a unique cryptographic key and mathematically encrypts your text locally. Absolutely no data is ever sent to or received from a server. The resulting encrypted payload is compressed and embedded entirely within the web URL itself. When a reader opens the link, their browser performs the local decompression and decryption instantly.

DISCLAIMER: This tool is free and open-source, meaning the website will never go down. However, any file or image shown on created pages is not hosted here; they are embedded from a 3rd party website by the creator of the link. We are not responsible for any content generated. Because all data is mathematically encrypted in the URL, there is no way for us to moderate or take down any pages.
